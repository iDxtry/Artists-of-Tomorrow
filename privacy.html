Privacy Policy

Data Collection

Artists of Tomorrow loads Microsoft Clarity and Google Analytics only after you accept analytics cookies. When enabled, these tools collect anonymous usage data about how visitors interact with our website so we can improve the user experience.

Information We Collect

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • General geographic location (country/city level)
  • Device information (browser, operating system, screen size)
  • Referral source (how you found our site)

How We Use This Data

We use this information to:

  • Improve our website design and functionality
  • Understand which competition information is most helpful
  • Identify any issues with the submission process
  • Ensure our site works well on all devices

Data Storage

When you accept analytics cookies, collected usage data is stored in Microsoft Clarity and Google Analytics systems. We do not sell this data.

Your Rights

You can opt out of tracking by:

  • Using browser privacy features or extensions
  • Declining cookies through our consent banner
  • Using private browsing mode

Contact Information

If you have questions about our privacy practices, please contact us at info.artistsoftomorrow@gmail.com
