Submit Your Art

Competition Registration

Ready to participate in the Artists of Tomorrow Youth Art Competition? Complete the registration form to receive your unique submission number before scanning and uploading your artwork.

Write the submission number visibly on the back of your paper alongside your name, the date, and your grade level so judges can match your physical artwork with your online registration.

Open Registration Form

Before You Submit

  1. Confirm you are 12-18 years old at the time of submission.
  2. Scan both sides of your paper so your artwork and writing supplement are included.
  3. Use only original work and avoid AI-generated or plagiarized content.
  4. Review the full competition rules before uploading your files.
